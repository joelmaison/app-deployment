Title: Giants of Africa 2025: Kigali Awaits as New Generation Arise
Source: Olympics.com
URL: https://www.olympics.com/en/news/giants-of-africa-2025-kigali-awaits-as-new-generation-arise

INTRODUCTION
The Giants of Africa Festival returns to Kigali, Rwanda, from July 25 to August 2, 2025, promising an expanded celebration of basketball, culture, and youth empowerment. The event builds on the success of its inaugural edition in 2023.

EVENT DETAILS
- Dates: July 25 – August 2, 2025
- Location: BK Arena and surrounding venues in Kigali
- Participants: 320 athletes (male and female) from 20 African countries
- Countries: Rwanda, Cameroon, South Sudan, Burkina Faso, Nigeria, Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Senegal, Mali, DR Congo, Morocco, Ivory Coast, Ghana, Somalia, Botswana, South Africa, Gabon, Benin, Ethiopia

KEY ACTIVITIES
- Basketball Tournament: Round-robin games leading to Championship and All-Star matches
- Training Clinics: NBA and WNBA coaches mentoring young athletes
- Women’s Empowerment Event: Community engagement across all participating countries
- Cultural Showcase: Fashion designers presenting collections to celebrate African heritage
- Entertainment: Performances by DJ Waffles and Nigerian artist Kizz Daniel at opening and closing ceremonies

INFRASTRUCTURE INITIATIVES
- Court Construction: Three new basketball courts unveiled in Kigali under the Giants of Africa “Built Within” initiative
- Coaching Development: 100 local coaches trained by NBA and WNBA personnel

SIGNIFICANCE
The festival emphasizes youth development, cultural exchange, and community building, reinforcing Rwanda’s role as a hub for African sports infrastructure.
