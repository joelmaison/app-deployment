Title: Giants of Africa Camps
Source: Giants of Africa Official Website
URL: https://giantsofafrica.org/what-we-do/camps/

INTRODUCTION
Since 2003, Giants of Africa has hosted basketball camps across the African continent for boys and girls, combining athletic training with life skills education.

PURPOSE
The camps aim to inspire youth to DREAM BIG, equipping them with basketball techniques and personal development tools to succeed on and off the court.

ACTIVITIES OFFERED
- Basketball training led by NBA personnel and local coaches.
- Life skills sessions focusing on leadership, discipline, and community impact.

IMPACT
Many camp alumni have achieved success in sports and diverse careers such as medicine, engineering, and entrepreneurship. The initiative fosters empowerment and unity among African youth.

COMMITMENTS
In 2021, Giants of Africa pledged to build 100 basketball courts across Africa to provide safe spaces for play and community engagement.
