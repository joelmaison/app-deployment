Title: The Rise of African Basketball
Source: Eugene Ekuban Blog
URL: https://www.eugeneekuban.space/the-rise-of-african-basketball

INTRODUCTION
The article examines the rapid growth of basketball across Africa, highlighting the influence of international partnerships, grassroots programs, and the emergence of professional leagues like BAL.

HISTORICAL CONTEXT
Basketball in Africa began gaining traction in the mid-20th century, but lacked infrastructure and investment. Recent decades have seen transformative changes through collaborations with FIBA, NBA Africa, and local federations.

KEY POINTS
- Expansion of professional leagues such as BAL.
- Increased youth engagement through grassroots initiatives.
- Role of technology and media in amplifying African basketball globally.

SOCIAL IMPACT
Basketball has become a tool for education, empowerment, and community development. Programs target gender equality and provide career pathways for young athletes.
