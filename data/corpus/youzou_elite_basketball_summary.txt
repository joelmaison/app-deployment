INTRODUCTION
Youzou Elite Basketball is a premier men's league in Gabon and Cameroon, featuring top-tier competitions and elite talent development across Zone 4 of FIBA Africa.

HIGHLIGHTS & UPDATES
- Gabon: National team finalised pre-selected players after two months of training.
- Cameroon: Seini Amadou earned a senior national team call-up after strong U18 performance.
- Tournaments: Gabon Cup, Cameroon Cup, Elite Women's Final 4 showcased top talent.
- Championships: Youzou Elite Men and Women Basketball finals held in Gabon.

KEY FACTS
- Region: FIBA Africa Zone 4
- Countries: Gabon, Cameroon, Central African Republic
- Focus: Grassroots development, elite competition, and talent scouting.
