Title: Basketball Africa League Announces Roster of Partners for Playoffs and Finals
Source: BAL Africa Newsroom
URL: https://bal.africa-newsroom.com/press/basketball-africa-league-announces-roster-of-partners-for-playoffs-and-finals-underway-now-in-south-africa?lang=en

INTRODUCTION
The Basketball Africa League (BAL) announced its roster of partners for the 2025 Playoffs and Finals, held at SunBet Arena in Pretoria, South Africa. This marks the first time the Playoffs are hosted in South Africa.

NEW PARTNERS
- Energade
- Hyundai
- Uber

EXISTING PARTNERS
- Rwanda Development Board
- NIKE
- Jordan Brand
- Wilson
- Afreximbank
- Castle Lite
- Hennessy
- RwandAir
- ServiceNow

EVENT DETAILS
- Semifinals: June 11, 2025
    * Al Ahli Tripoli (Libya) vs APR (Rwanda) – 4:00 p.m. CAT
    * Al Ittihad (Egypt) vs Petro de Luanda (Angola) – 7:00 p.m. CAT
- Finals: June 14, 2025

PARTNER ACTIVATIONS
- Energade: Official Sports Drink Partner; fan engagement in BAL Fan Zone.
- Hyundai: Vehicle displays and merchandise giveaways.
- Uber: In-arena food ordering via Uber Eats; special drop-off zone.
- Afreximbank: BAL Advance program for player development.
- Castle Lite: On-court and in-arena fan experiences.
- Hennessy: Premium courtside experience at The Hennessy Lounge.
- NIKE: Distribution of NIKE A’One sneakers at BAL4Her U-23 camp.
