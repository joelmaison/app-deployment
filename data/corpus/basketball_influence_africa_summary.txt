INTRODUCTION
Basketball’s popularity in Africa continues to rise, according to new data from NBA Africa. The report highlights significant growth in fan engagement, digital reach, and merchandise sales, signaling the sport’s expanding footprint across the continent.

KEY METRICS
- Viewership: Live game views surged by 35% year-over-year, driven by BAL broadcasts and NBA Africa initiatives.
- Social Media Growth: NBA Africa’s followers grew by 50%, surpassing 5 million across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, X, and YouTube.
- Merchandise Sales: Official NBA and BAL merchandise sales increased by 40%, reflecting growing fan loyalty.

CONTRIBUTING FACTORS
- Basketball Africa League (BAL): Expanded coverage and competitive play attract new audiences.
- NBA Africa Programs: Jr. NBA clinics, BAL4HER, and grassroots initiatives foster youth participation.
- Digital Strategy: Enhanced streaming options and localized content boost accessibility.

FUTURE OUTLOOK
NBA Africa aims to deepen engagement through tech-driven fan experiences, more localized events, and partnerships with African broadcasters. The league’s growth trajectory positions basketball as a major sport in Africa’s evolving sports economy.
