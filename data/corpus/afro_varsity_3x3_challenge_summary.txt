Title: 5th Afro Varsity 3x3 Basketball Challenge
Source: Africa University Sports
URL: https://africauniversitysports.com/14-universities-set-to-compete-in-5th-afro-varsity-3x3-basketball-challenge-at-university-of-johannesburg/

INTRODUCTION
The 5th Afro Varsity 3x3 Basketball Challenge was scheduled to take place at the University of Johannesburg, South Africa, from April 27th to 30th, 2023. The event aimed to bring together top university teams from across Africa.

REASON FOR POSTPONEMENT
The tournament was postponed due to failure to meet the quorum of eight countries required for a FISU qualifying event. Only five countries confirmed participation: Uganda, Egypt, Zambia, South Africa, and Kenya.

SIGNIFICANCE
The Afro Varsity 3x3 Basketball Challenge serves as a key platform for African university athletes to showcase talent and qualify for the FISU University World Cup 3x3 Basketball.

FUTURE OUTLOOK
Organizers hope to reschedule the event soon, ensuring broader participation and maintaining the integrity of the competition.
