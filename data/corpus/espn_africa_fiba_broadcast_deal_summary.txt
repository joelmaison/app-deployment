Title: ESPN Africa Secures Exclusive FIBA Broadcast Rights Across Sub-Saharan Africa
Source: ESPN
URL: https://www.espn.co.uk/espn/story/_/id/47104972/espn-africa-broadcast-international-basketball-new-fiba-deal

INTRODUCTION
ESPN Africa has signed a four-year agreement with FIBA to broadcast international basketball games across sub-Saharan Africa. This landmark deal strengthens ESPN Africa’s position as the premier destination for basketball fans on the continent.

KEY DETAILS OF THE AGREEMENT
- Duration: 2025–2029
- Scope: Exclusive broadcast rights for FIBA competitions in sub-Saharan Africa
- Coverage: Over 1,500 international basketball games

TOURNAMENTS COVERED
- FIBA Basketball World Cups (Men’s and Women’s)
- Continental Cups (including FIBA AfroBasket)
- Youth World Cups
- Olympic Qualifying Events

SIGNIFICANCE
This partnership ensures African fans access to top-tier global basketball content, showcasing elite international teams and rising stars. It also highlights African talent on the global stage, reinforcing basketball’s growing popularity across the continent.

- Nov 30: Cameroon vs South Sudan (12:00 CAT), Libya vs Cape Verde (15:00 CAT), Guinea vs Tunisia (18:00 CAT), Nigeria vs Rwanda (21:00 CAT)
