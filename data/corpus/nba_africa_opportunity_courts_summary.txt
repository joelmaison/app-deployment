Title: NBA Africa and Opportunity International Unveil New Basketball Courts in Rwanda and Kenya
Source: Opportunity International
URL: https://opportunity.org/news/press-releases/nba-africa-and-opportunity-international-unveil-new-basketball-courts

INTRODUCTION
On May 19, 2025, NBA Africa and Opportunity International announced the unveiling of new outdoor basketball courts in Rwanda and Kenya. This initiative supports NBA Africa’s commitment to build 1,000 courts across Africa over the next decade.

EVENT DETAILS
- First Court: Highland School, Nyamata, Rwanda (May 19, 2025)
- Second Court: Loiswell Academy, Nairobi, Kenya (May 27, 2025)
- Ribbon-cutting ceremony held during BAL Nile Conference group phase.
- Attendees: NBA Africa CEO Clare Akamanzi, BAL President Amadou Gallo Fall, Opportunity International EVP Randy Kurtz, and BAL Ambassador Luol Deng.

OBJECTIVES
- Provide safe spaces for boys and girls to learn and play basketball.
- Foster confidence, teamwork, and community engagement.
- Support Opportunity International’s mission to improve education outcomes and NBA Africa’s vision to expand basketball access.

KEY ACTIVITIES
- Jr. NBA/Jr. WNBA clinic for 80 youth (ages 16 and under).
- Coaching clinic for 50 local coaches on the new court.
- Courts equipped with new hoops and changing facilities.

IMPACT METRICS
- NBA Africa reached 350,000 youth through development programs last year.
- Opportunity International has funded 19,000 schools, benefiting 16 million children globally.

ORGANIZATIONAL BACKGROUND
- NBA Africa: Operates BAL, promotes youth and elite development, and expands basketball access across all 54 African countries.
- Opportunity International: Global nonprofit providing financial resources and education support to 21 million families worldwide.
