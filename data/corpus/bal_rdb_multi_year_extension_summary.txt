Title: BAL and Rwanda Development Board Announce Multi-Year Extension to Host Games in Kigali
Source: Rwanda Development Board (RDB)
URL: https://rdb.rw/basketball-africa-league-and-rwanda-development-board-announce-multi-year-extension-to-play-bal-games-in-kigali/

INTRODUCTION
On June 19, 2023, the Basketball Africa League (BAL) and Rwanda Development Board (RDB) announced a multi-year extension of their partnership to host BAL games at BK Arena in Kigali, Rwanda. The agreement reinforces Rwanda’s position as a continental hub for basketball and sports tourism.

KEY DETAILS OF THE AGREEMENT
- BAL will continue to stage Playoffs and Finals in Kigali for 2024, 2026, and 2028.
- Collaboration includes youth development and social impact programs.
- “Visit Rwanda” remains a Foundational and Host Partner of BAL.
- RwandAir continues as Official Airline Partner, facilitating team and staff travel.

OBJECTIVES
- Promote Rwanda as a premier destination for sports and tourism.
- Strengthen grassroots basketball through Jr. NBA, Special Olympics, and coaching clinics.
- Advance gender equality via BAL4HER initiatives.
- Support innovation and entrepreneurship through BAL Innovation Summits.

PARTNERS INVOLVED
- Rwanda Development Board (RDB)
- RwandAir
- BAL Foundational Partners: NIKE, Jordan Brand, Wilson, New Fortress Energy
- Marketing Partners: Hennessy, Attorney General Alliance (AGA-Africa), Envol City, Société Pétroliere (SP)

SIGNIFICANCE
The partnership underscores Rwanda’s commitment to hosting world-class sporting events, driving economic growth, and creating job opportunities while showcasing Made in Rwanda products and cultural heritage.
