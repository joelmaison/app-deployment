Title: Basketball Africa League and AFD Advance Education and Inclusion in Africa
Source: Agence Française de Développement (AFD)
URL: https://www.afd.fr/fr/actualites/communique-de-presse/basketball-africa-league-et-afd-promeuvent-education-et-inclusion-afrique

INTRODUCTION
The Basketball Africa League (BAL) and Agence Française de Développement (AFD) announced a strategic partnership to promote education, gender equality, and social inclusion through basketball across Africa. This collaboration aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

OBJECTIVES
- Use basketball as a tool for social development and empowerment.
- Improve access to education and well-being for disadvantaged communities.
- Promote gender equality and inclusion of girls and women at all levels of society.

KEY INITIATIVES
- Sport en commun: A pan-African platform launched by AFD to finance and support sports projects that foster economic and social development.
- Youth Summit: BAL and AFD will organize the African Youth Summit in Dakar to address socio-economic challenges and explore solutions through sports.
- Capacity Building: Provide resources, skills, and opportunities to young Africans beyond the basketball court.

IMPACT AREAS
- Education: Integrating basketball programs with educational initiatives.
- Gender Equality: Empowering women and girls through BAL4HER and related programs.
- Social Development: Strengthening community cohesion and creating pathways for youth leadership.

NOTABLE QUOTES
- Rémy Rioux, CEO of AFD: “This partnership will make sport a lever for sustainable development in Africa.”
- Amadou Gallo Fall, President of BAL: “We launched BAL to grow the game in Africa, but its impact goes far beyond the court.”

SIGNIFICANCE
The partnership demonstrates how sports can drive sustainable development, offering young Africans opportunities to thrive socially and economically.
