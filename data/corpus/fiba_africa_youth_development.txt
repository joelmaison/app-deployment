Title: FIBA Africa – Youth Development Initiatives
Source: FIBA Official Website
URL: https://about.fiba.basketball/en/regions/africa/activities/youth-development

INTRODUCTION
The page outlines FIBA Africa’s commitment to nurturing young basketball talent through structured programs, competitions, and educational initiatives across the continent.

HISTORICAL CONTEXT
Youth development has been a cornerstone of FIBA Africa’s mission since its inception, aiming to create sustainable pathways for players from grassroots to elite levels.

KEY POINTS
- Organization of U16 and U18 continental championships.
- Implementation of Basketball Schools and training camps.
- Focus on coaching education and referee development for youth competitions.

SOCIAL IMPACT
These programs empower young athletes, promote gender equality, and foster life skills through sport. They also strengthen community engagement and educational opportunities.

NOTABLE EXAMPLES
- FIBA Africa Youth Camps held in multiple regions.
- Rising stars identified through U16 and U18 tournaments progressing to BAL and international leagues.

LOOKING AHEAD
Plans include expanding digital learning platforms, increasing grassroots outreach, and enhancing partnerships to support youth basketball infrastructure.
