Title: FIBA Africa Youth Competitions
Source: FIBA Official Website
URL: https://about.fiba.basketball/en/regions/africa/competitions/youth-competitions

INTRODUCTION
FIBA Africa Youth Competitions provide a platform for young basketball talent to compete at the highest regional level and develop their skills under expert guidance.

COMPETITIONS
- FIBA U16 AfroBasket (Boys)
- FIBA U16 Women’s AfroBasket
- FIBA U18 AfroBasket (Boys)
- FIBA U18 Women’s AfroBasket

QUALIFICATION DETAILS
- These tournaments serve as qualifiers for the FIBA U17 World Cup and FIBA U19 World Cup.
- Top two boys’ and girls’ teams progress to global youth events.
- Third and fourth placed girls’ teams qualify for next AfroBasket youth events.

PURPOSE
- Promote basketball growth in Africa.
- Identify and nurture future stars.
- Provide opportunities for athletes to learn from experienced coaches and represent their countries.
