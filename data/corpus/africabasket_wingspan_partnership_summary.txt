Title: AfricaBasket Partners with WINGSPAN Scouting to Scout Africa’s Next Basketball Superstars
Source: AfricaBasket
URL: https://www.africabasket.net/articles/africabasket-partners-with-wingspan-to-scout-africas-next-basketball-superstars

INTRODUCTION
AfricaBasket announced a strategic partnership with WINGSPAN Scouting, Africa’s leading basketball scouting service, to identify and nurture top basketball talent across the continent. This collaboration aims to elevate African basketball and provide visibility for emerging players.

PURPOSE OF THE COLLABORATION
- Create a platform for evaluating and promoting African basketball talent.
- Support pathways for players to reach NBA teams, colleges, and FIBA clubs.
- Share scouting insights and player development strategies with the basketball community.

BACKGROUND ON WINGSPAN SCOUTING
- Founded: 2023 by Jacques Vandescure and David Helmer.
- Mission: Recognize and track young talent from formative years.
- Expertise: Jacques Vandescure, former NBA scout for San Antonio Spurs, brings global experience and deep knowledge of player evaluation.

KEY INSIGHTS FROM JACQUES VANDESCURE
- Scouting philosophy emphasizes skill development, mental preparation, and cultural understanding.
- Focus on empowering African players to explore global opportunities while fostering growth within the continent.
- WINGSPAN aims to provide positive feedback and visibility to players, boosting confidence and motivation.

EXPECTED IMPACT
- Strengthen African basketball infrastructure through professional scouting.
- Highlight rising stars from grassroots programs and regional camps.
- Foster collaborations that promote African basketball culture and global competitiveness.

CITATIONS
