Title: NBA Africa Partners with Opportunity International to Build Courts and Empower Youth
Source: EduFinance
URL: https://edufinance.org/latest/news/nba-africa-partnership

INTRODUCTION
On October 8, 2024, NBA Africa and Opportunity International announced a strategic partnership to enhance basketball infrastructure and youth development in Africa. The collaboration focuses on building outdoor courts and conducting youth clinics in Nairobi, Kenya, and Kigali, Rwanda.

OBJECTIVES
- Support NBA Africa’s commitment to construct 1,000 basketball courts across Africa within the next decade, including 100 in Kenya.
- Provide safe spaces for youth to play and learn leadership skills through basketball.

KEY INITIATIVES
- Court Construction: Outdoor courts in Nairobi and Kigali.
- Youth Clinics: Grassroots programs teaching basketball fundamentals and life skills.
- Coach Development: Training for coaches and teachers in refereeing, game operations, event management, and program administration.

JR. NBA IMPACT
- Jr. NBA program reached over 170,000 youth across Africa last year.
- Focus on teaching core values and fundamentals of basketball at the grassroots level.

NBA AFRICA’S BROADER MISSION
- Operates Basketball Africa League (BAL) and promotes elite and youth development.
- Offices in Cairo, Dakar, Johannesburg, Lagos, and Nairobi.
- Expands access to basketball through media distribution, corporate partnerships, and social responsibility programs.

SIGNIFICANCE
This partnership reflects NBA Africa’s long-term vision to invest in local ecosystems and empower the next generation of African athletes and leaders.

CITATIONS
